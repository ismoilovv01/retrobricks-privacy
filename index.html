Legal · Privacy Policy

Retro Bricks

This app does not collect any personal data

Overview

Retro Bricks is a classic brick-breaking arcade game. We take your privacy seriously — which is why we built it to work entirely on your device, with no data ever leaving it.

Retro Bricks does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. All game data stays on your device.

Data We Collect

We collect nothing. Specifically:

  • No name, email address, or account information
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No usage analytics or crash reports sent to our servers
  • No camera, microphone, or contacts access
  • No financial or payment information

Local Storage

The app saves your high scores, game progress, and settings locally on your device using Android's built-in storage (Room database). This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's data from your Android settings.

Internet Access

Retro Bricks does not request internet permission and does not make any network connections. The app functions fully offline.

Third-Party Services

Retro Bricks does not integrate any third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or social features. There are no embedded trackers of any kind.

Children's Privacy

Because we collect no data whatsoever, Retro Bricks is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. We are compliant with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) by design — there is simply no data to collect or share.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will post the new version on this page and update the "Last updated" date above. Any material changes would be announced via a Play Store update notice.
